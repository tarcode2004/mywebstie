Digital Research Portfolio by Tarik Metin

I spent my junior year in high school researching Artificial Intelligence as a part of the Intern Mentor class. I had heard a lot about the technology, however, I did not know much about it. After researching different machine learning algorithms, neural networks, and the state of AI, I interviewed 4 AI experts at UMBC about the capabilities, limitations, and state of AI in general. Key Aspects of My Research

Capabilities(Strenghts and Weaknesses)

My research discusses the strenghts and key weaknesses of AI that is preventing AI from being applied to more technologies.

Transparency and Ethics

My research discusses the reoccuring issue of transpareny in the field of AI and analyzes how a technology that is not understood can have ethically wrong outcomes.

Sustainability

My research analyzes the current trend and pricing of AI.

Availability

My research also analyzes access to AI technology.
